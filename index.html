About Me

Hello! I'm Shweta Pandey, a PhD student at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) in Bangalore. My research focuses on the development of high-performance systems that integrate the robust capabilities of both CPUs and GPUs, alongside various types of memory subsystems. My passion lies in optimizing these technologies to create more efficient and powerful computational architectures. Through my work, I aim to push the boundaries of what's possible in system performance, making significant contributions to the field of computing.
